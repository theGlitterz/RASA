About RĀSA

RĀSA reimagines fragrance as an everyday companion with Perfume Charms —portable, elegant, and compact yet luxurious, the charms blend practicality with artistry.

The inspiration comes from Navrasa, the nine essential emotions of Indian art and philosophy—joy, love, wonder, peace, courage, anger, compassion, fear, and disgust. Just as each rasa represents a different shade of human experience, every RĀSA Perfume Charm embodies a mood, allowing wearers to carry their chosen emotion with them throughout the day.

RĀSA perfume charms clip easily onto bags, keys, or accessories, transforming fragrance into a wearable, personal ritual that’s always within reach.
