About RASA

We design perfume as a companion — a refined charm that clips to your bag and travels with you. Refillable. Responsible. Remarkably you.

Each piece is engineered for micro-misting, minimizing waste while maximizing sillage. Materials are selected for longevity, with finishes that patinate beautifully over time.

Hand-finished details, considered ergonomics, and signature accords define our approach.
